### January Recap of the ESCS Community Forum

**News & Resources 📰**

- **A new paper published on PLOS: Eleven Quick Tips for Organizing a Data Cleaning Challenge.** Authored by Ireen J. Kal, Lidwien P. Smabers, Joanna von Berg, Giulia Perticari, Mette van de Meent, Denise Fokkelman, Joep Sprangers, Loïc Lannelongue, and Florijn Dekkers, this resource is most helpful to researchers and practitioners implementing digital sustainability and applying to Green DiSC in their institutions. [Download the paper here.](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1013791)

**Jobs & Funding Opportunities 📣**

- **Deadline for applications for NetDRIVE's Summer School for UK PhD students closes on the 1st of February.** Hosted at the University of Durham from 15-19 June 2026, NetDRIVE is running a fully-funded summer school on making positive environmental change in computation and data research practices within large scale compute and HPC. [Learn more here](https://forum.escs-community.org/t/funded-summer-school-for-uk-phd-students-making-positive-environmental-change-in-computational-and-data-research-practices-within-large-scale-compute-and-hpc/237) and [register on the website here](https://durham.readthedocs.io/en/latest/netdrive/index.html). <br>
    👉 *[Join the discussion on the forum](https://forum.escs-community.org/t/funded-summer-school-for-uk-phd-students-making-positive-environmental-change-in-computational-and-data-research-practices-within-large-scale-compute-and-hpc/237)*

**Upcoming Events 🎟️**

- **SC4RC 2026, the first interdisciplinary conference on environmentally sustainable research computing from 4-8 May in CERN, Geneva, Switzerland is now open for early bird registration and abstract submission.** Early bird registration is extended to 06 February and all registrations will close by the 10th of April. Abstracts are open for submission until the 27th of February. Learn more on the [website](https://www.sc4rc.org/) and through [this link](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1526482). <br>
    👉 *[Join the discussion on the forum](https://forum.escs-community.org/t/excited-to-announce-sc4rc-2026-the-first-interdisciplinary-conference-on-environmentally-sustainable-research-computing-happening-in-geneva-switzerland-on-4-8-may-2026/219)*

- **CECAM-CSCS is running EcoCompute: Building Sustainable Scientific Computing Practices through Academia-Industry Collaboration.** This workshop on environmental sustainability will run from 5-7 May in CSCS, Lugano, Switzerland. There are 45 slots available for face-to-face participation, while online participation is unlimited. [Register here](https://www.cecam.org/workshop-details/ecocompute-building-sustainable-scientific-computing-practices-through-academia-industry-collaboration-1475). <br>
    👉 *[Join the discussion on the forum](https://forum.escs-community.org/t/ecocompute-cecam-cscs-workshop-5-7-may-2026-lugano-switzerland-and-online/242)*

- **Online training workshop on advocating for and implementing sustainable digital strategies.** JISC is running online workshops on the 29th of April and the 16th of July. [See pricing and eligibility here](https://www.jisc.ac.uk/training/driving-digital-sustainability).

**Q&A Discussions 💬**

- **New induction material resource for Green Computing** For those working on Green DiSC, Alessio Locallo has shared their team’s guidelines for responsible usage of computational resources on the HPC in [this thread](https://forum.escs-community.org/t/induction-material-for-green-computing/184/8). <br>
    👉 *[Join the discussion on the forum](https://forum.escs-community.org/t/induction-material-for-green-computing/184/8)*

### Help us grow the ESCS Community with others working on sustainable computing! 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

If you haven’t already joined, the [ESCS Forum](https://forum.escs-community.org/categories) is a great place to connect with others, whether you’re new to the space or have been in this domain for years. 🌱 ***What is this newsletter?*** *This aims to be a round up of interesting news and developments in the sustainable computing space. Whenever we come across an interesting news item or publication on the topic, we will post it on the forum so that everyone can jump in and discuss it. Of course anyone’s welcome to post as well! At the end of each month, we will send out this monthly digest of the articles shared and of the discussions that happened on the forum.*

🌱 ***Who is behind the forum and this monthly digest?*** *This project is led by [Loïc Lannelongue](https://www.lannelongue-group.org/members/Loic-Lannelongue.html) at the University of Cambridge and supported by the Wellcome Trust (among others). [Anica Araneta](https://www.lannelongue-group.org/members/Anica-Araneta.html) is in charge of day-to-day community management. Any questions about that? [Just get in touch](contact@escs-community.org)!*
